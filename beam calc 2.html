BEAM CALCULATOR

10Digit SLS Estimator
by LOUIE LUMEN

Use this calculator to determine either the size of the circle of light, how far away you should place the light, or which degree of lens you should use. Put in two of the values and then hit calculate to find the third value.

Values can be in feet or meters, but both values must match.
